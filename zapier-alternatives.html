The real reasons
Why people leave Zapier
Based on analysis of 3,043 verified Capterra reviews and Trustpilot complaints — these are the most cited switching triggers in 2026.
Reason #1
Pricing jumps aggressively
Zapier's free plan allows only 100 tasks/month. Once you cross that, pricing escalates fast — teams report bills going from $0 to $49/month overnight as automation usage grows.
Reason #2
Complex multi-step workflows
Simple 2-3 step automations work well. But multi-step Zaps with conditional logic, data transformation, and multiple data flows become cumbersome and hard to debug.
Reason #3
Poor customer support & no refunds
Multiple verified reviews cite inflexible no-refund policies, rude support agents, and unresponsive customer service — even for long-term customers with legitimate complaints.
Reason #4
Task counting is confusing
Zapier counts each action in a multi-step Zap as a separate task. A 5-step automation uses 5 tasks per run — catching many users off guard when bills arrive.
Reason #5
Better value alternatives exist
Make.com, n8n, and Pabbly Connect offer more operations per month at a fraction of Zapier's cost — with more powerful visual workflow builders.
Established alternatives
6 proven tools people switch to
Ranked by switching frequency — not paid placement.
Make.com
Visual automation builder — more powerful, far cheaper than Zapier
Most switched toFree plan
Make.com (formerly Integromat) is the most common Zapier replacement in 2026. Visual drag-and-drop scenario builder, 1,000 operations/month free (vs Zapier's 100), and significantly cheaper at scale. Handles complex multi-step workflows, conditional routing, and data transformation far more elegantly than Zapier's linear Zap model.
Best for
Teams outgrowing Zapier's task limits or needing complex conditional workflows — Make handles both at 5-10x lower cost.
Free (1,000 ops/mo) · Paid from $9/month
Visit Make.com
n8n
Open-source, self-hosted automation — unlimited workflows, zero per-task cost
Free self-hostedOpen source
n8n is the self-hosted alternative that completely eliminates Zapier's task-based pricing. Run unlimited automations on your own server — no per-task charges, ever. The visual workflow builder is powerful, supports custom code nodes, and has 400+ integrations. Multiple Shopify reviews specifically cite switching to n8n as the solution to Zapier's cost and reliability problems.
Best for
Technical teams and developers who want unlimited automations without per-task pricing and full data ownership on their own infrastructure.
Free self-hosted · Cloud from $20/month
Visit n8n
Pabbly Connect
Unlimited workflows — one flat price, no task caps
Paid
Pabbly Connect's pricing model is the direct answer to Zapier's task-counting frustration — one flat monthly fee, unlimited workflows, unlimited tasks. No surprise bills as your automation grows. 1,000+ app integrations, multi-step workflows, and a lifetime deal option that makes it one of the most cost-effective Zapier alternatives available.
Best for
Teams that run high-volume automations and need predictable pricing without per-task charges eating into margins.
From $19/month · Lifetime deal available
Visit Pabbly
ActivePieces
Open-source Zapier alternative — self-host or cloud
Free planOpen source
ActivePieces is the fastest-growing open-source Zapier alternative in 2026. Clean Zapier-like interface but self-hostable, with a growing library of community-built pieces (integrations). Free cloud plan available, and the self-hosted version is completely free with no limits. Actively developed with new integrations added weekly.
Best for
Teams who want Zapier's simplicity but with open-source flexibility, self-hosting capability, and no vendor lock-in.
Free plan · Cloud from $19/month
Visit ActivePieces
Microsoft Power Automate
Enterprise automation bundled with Microsoft 365
Included in M365
For teams already on Microsoft 365, Power Automate is included at no extra cost. Deep integration with Teams, SharePoint, Outlook, and all Office apps. More complex than Zapier to set up, but for Microsoft-heavy organizations it eliminates the need for a separate automation tool entirely.
Best for
Organizations already on Microsoft 365 who want automation without an additional subscription — Power Automate is already included.
Included in M365 · Standalone from $15/user/month
Visit Power Automate
Pipedream
Developer-first automation with code + no-code hybrid
Free planDev favourite
Pipedream sits between no-code tools like Zapier and full custom code. You can use pre-built triggers and actions, but also drop into Node.js, Python, or Go at any step. 1,000 free invocations/day, generous free tier, and the ability to handle complex data transformations that Zapier simply can't.
Best for
Developers and technical teams who need the flexibility to write custom code within their automation workflows.
Free (1,000/day) · Paid from $29/month
Visit Pipedream
