The real reasons
Why teams leave CleverTap
Based on analysis of verified G2, Capterra and TrustRadius reviews — these are the most consistent switching triggers in 2026.
Reason #1
Pricing escalates fast
CleverTap's pricing starts around $72/month for 5,000 MAU and scales steeply with no transparent self-serve evaluation path. Teams regularly report sticker shock as their user base grows.
Reason #2
Flawed MAU calculation
Verified TrustRadius reviewers flag CleverTap's MAU counting as unfair — users who open an email (without visiting the app) are counted as active, inflating bills beyond actual app engagement.
Reason #3
Outdated interface
G2 reviewers in 2026 consistently cite the UI as feeling dated and overwhelming for new users — filtering and viewing users requires setting a condition first, limiting ad-hoc exploration.
Reason #4
No data warehouse integration
Teams whose behavioral data lives in Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift hit a hard wall — CleverTap lacks native data warehouse integration, forcing expensive ETL layers.
Reason #5
Message deliverability issues
Multiple verified reviews report notifications not reliably reaching end users — a critical failure for a platform whose core job is message delivery across push, email, SMS, and in-app.
