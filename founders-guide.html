Product, Design & Engineering

The Builder Archetype

FigmaUI/UX Design

The industry default standard tool for collaborative wireframing, component libraries, and validating early high-fidelity workflows without code commitment.

LinearIssue Tracking

A blazingly fast alternative to legacy tools. Stripped of bloat, it enables engineers to focus exclusively on product cycles and continuous deployment workflows.

CursorAI Development

An AI-first code fork environment built natively on top of VS Code, accelerating deep iteration speeds and simplifying boilerplate code generation.

Vercel / RenderMVP Deployment

Cloud architecture engines that simplify the continuous integration pipeline, instantly resolving front-end deployments and small stack environments with zero specialized DevOps overhead.

Marketing & Growth

The Storyteller Archetype

Framer / WebflowSite Generation

Visual development solutions that output production-grade marketing funnels and conversion-optimized informational assets without relying on web teams.

CanvaVisual Assets

A highly scalable layout platform designed to produce brand-compliant presentation materials, marketing collaterals, and targeted social ad creatives quickly.

Perplexity / AhrefsMarket Intel

Advanced systems configured to evaluate structural SEO trends, competitive digital foot-printing, and high-intent industry content opportunities.

An elastic email orchestration utility built to support subscriber distribution growth and structure simple nurture campaigns for inbound validation funnels.

Sales & CRM

The Hustler Archetype

Apollo.ioB2B Prospecting

Operations, Finance & Legal

The Back Office Archetype

NotionKnowledge Base

The internal systemic single source of truth used to institutionalize documentation, engineering spec sheets, and cross-functional operations.

Stripe Atlas + MercuryCorporate Rails

A highly integrated ecosystem designed to launch entities cleanly, manage cap tables, and settle transactions via startup-centric fiscal interfaces.

QuickBooks / XeroAccounting Ledger

Strict baseline accounting systems that insulate company ledgers early, preventing costly audit cleanups during subsequent venture financing stages.

Deel / RipplingGlobal Workforce

Automated contract distribution structures that simplify border-free hiring compliance and manage international contractor payroll effortlessly.

💡 Operational Rule of Thumb: Postpone committing capital to any specialized stack subscription layer until the absence of that automation costs your core operation more than an hour of manual tracking every week. Lean heavily on tier allowances during initial validation phases.
